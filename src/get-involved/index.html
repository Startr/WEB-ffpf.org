Make A Difference


Sometimes, it’s hard to know where to begin when you want to help. You may not be able to join us at our events, but that doesn’t mean you can’t make an impact. A simple donation can bring relief and hope to those in need, showing them they are not alone.

Every contribution, big or small, goes directly toward purchasing essential medications and medical supplies. These resources save lives, ease suffering, and give vulnerable individuals a chance at a better tomorrow. Your kindness can make all the difference It Starts With You.

BE A VOLUNTEER

WE NEED YOU

It is thanks to our many volunteers that we are able to make a difference. Our team is made up of a wide range of humanitarian workers:

  • Nurses
  • Occupational therapists
  • Doctors
  • Accountants
  • Students and many others.

All have in common the determination to make a significant difference in the daily life of the less fortunate and to help them build a better future.

These people dedicate themselves with humanism, generosity and empathy so that the families of this region can aspire to a better life.

You want to join our team? Contact us now.

Email address: vdargan@videotron.ca

WHO CAN APPLY?

Students as well as professionals are always welcome to work with us. Persons having experience in healthcare and humanitarian activities and other NGO related experience will be preferred.

WHAT ARE THE BENEFITS?

Volunteers participating in our activities will be provided the relevant experience letters and recommendations and will be able to become a part of someone’s life changing treatments.

HOW TO APPLY?

STEP 1

CONTACT US

Write to us if you would like to part of the great humanitarian work.

STEP 2

TELEPHONIC DISCUSSION

To get to know you, we will have an initial discussion on telephone to understand your motivation and relevant experiences.

STEP 3

FACE TO FACE DISCUSSION

After the telephonic discussion we would invite you for a coffee or tea to discuss about the opportunities that we currently have or might have in the future.

STEP 4

START THE WORK

Welcome to our team! Help us to help others.

FUNDRAISING

Fundraising Dinner - Freedom from Poverty Foundation

Every November - 7985 Maurice-Duplessis Blvd., Montreal

DETAILS

Join us for an inspiring evening dedicated to transforming lives across India. Discover the passionate and compassionate individuals who are committed to forging a brighter future for underprivileged communities, starting in Jamkhed and now expanding their mission pan-India. Together, we are building a movement to break the cycle of poverty and provide life-changing opportunities to those who need them most.

This fundraising dinner is more than just an event—it’s a celebration of culture, hope, and collective impact. Indulge in authentic regional cuisine, enjoy an open bar, and dance the night away to vibrant beats from our DJ. Every contribution made during this evening will directly fund future initiatives, from surgical missions to empowering families with the tools to rewrite their stories. Your support has the power to spark change, and we can’t wait to share this incredible journey with you!
