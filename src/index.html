Breaking The Poverty Cycle Of The Most Disadvantaged

Together, We Can Transform Lives Across India

The Freedom from Poverty Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to breaking the cycle of poverty in communities across India. Through surgical, educational, and community-based initiatives, we aim to empower families and create opportunities for sustainable growth and independence.

Every dollar you contribute directly impacts underserved communities, improving their quality of life and helping them achieve long-term resilience and self-reliance.

Our mission is powered by a global network of dedicated volunteers, including doctors, nurses, occupational therapists, public health professionals, accountants, students, and experts from diverse fields. These passionate individuals generously share their time and skills to help us realize our vision of “breaking the cycle of poverty for the most disadvantaged across India.”
