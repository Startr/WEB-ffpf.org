## SLIDE 8 — What Happens Next

The next camps are already being planned.

**In September, October, and November of this year, our international teams will be running three consecutive camps.**

**$12,000 sponsors one full surgical camp.**

That is the cost of a week of transformation. A surgical team. Supplies. Patient transport. Post-operative care. Everything.

One camp. One sponsor. One week that changes lives that would otherwise never be reached.

This is not a donation. This is a **camp sponsorship** — your name, your legacy, attached to a specific mission in a specific place for specific patients.

<!--
SPEAKER NOTES:
Lead with the three-camp announcement — it shows momentum and urgency. Then the ask: $12,000 per camp. Say the number once, clearly, without apology. Explain what it buys in concrete terms: "Surgical supplies. Patient transport. Post-operative care. A full week." Frame it as a sponsorship, not a donation. These donors understand the difference. A donation disappears into a budget. A sponsorship has their name on it. Three camps means three sponsorship opportunities — let people do the math in their heads. (2 minutes)
